# 🌿 MIDORIA 👑

### Where every choice grows a greener kingdom.

MIDORIA is a sustainability awareness web application that helps users understand, track, and improve their environmental impact through personalized insights, visual reports, and actionable recommendations.

Rather than presenting raw carbon footprint values alone, MIDORIA transforms environmental data into a kingdom health system, making sustainability more engaging and understandable.

---

## 🌍 Problem Statement

Many individuals are unaware of how daily habits contribute to their carbon footprint.

Transportation, electricity consumption, diet choices, and water usage all affect environmental sustainability, but these impacts are often difficult to visualize and interpret.

MIDORIA aims to bridge this gap through a simple and interactive assessment experience.

## 🤖 Haruka AI Guide

Haruka serves as MIDORIA's environmental advisor.

Based on the user's assessment, Haruka identifies the largest environmental contributor and provides personalized recommendations for improvement.

---

## 📊 How MIDORIA Works

Users provide information regarding:

- Transportation habits
- Daily travel distance
- Electricity consumption
- Diet preferences
- Water usage

MIDORIA then:

1. Calculates estimated environmental impact
2. Generates a Kingdom Health Score
3. Determines Kingdom Status
4. Visualizes impact distribution
5. Provides personalized recommendations
6. Assigns a sustainability quest
